privacy policy

VIANET-ACCOUNT is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. As a result, we respect the laws and principles governing data protection and wish to inform you of how your personal information is collected and used.

By "your personal information" we mean any information about you provided by you or a third party, and that can identify you, such as your name, address, telephone number, email address as well as information necessary for commercial transactions.

The VIANET-ACCOUNT Privacy Principles, set out below, explain how VIANET-ACCOUNT will process your personal data.

VIANET-ACCOUNT Privacy Principles

  • We will only collect and use your personal information if we have legitimate reasons for doing so, in accordance with applicable regulations.
  • We will be transparent in our dealings with you and tell you how we will collect and use your personal information.
  • If we have collected your personal information for a particular reason, we will not use it for any other reason unless we inform you in advance and obtain your permission.
  • We only ask for the information we need.
  • We will update our files when you notify us of changes in your information.
  • We will continue to evaluate and update the quality of our information.
  • We will implement and comply with data storage measures related to your personal information and will ensure that your personal data will be destroyed safely at the end of the appropriate storage period.
  • We will respect the rights guaranteed to you under the privacy laws and your data in force and will ensure that queries related to security matters are handled quickly and with maximum transparency.
  • We will train our staff to respect these privacy requirements.
  • We will ensure that appropriate physical and technological security measures are put in place to protect your personal information, regardless of where it is stored.
  • We will ensure that when we outsource a service, the third party provider will have appropriate security measures in place and will require us, in the form of a contract, to enforce these privacy principles.
  • We guarantee that effective security measures will be in place when transferring personal information to other countries.

How information about you will be used

If you register to use our services, we will ask you to provide certain information about you for security, identification and verification purposes. The law requires us to obtain this information before allowing you to use the services we offer. When you provide information about other people (for example, if you "refer a friend") you must guarantee that you have their agreement or that you have the right to provide us with this information.

We will keep the information collected about you as long as it is permitted to do so for legitimate business purposes and in accordance with our data retention policy.

Your personal data (including sensitive personal data) may be shared with other financial institutions, commercial information agencies, professional bodies, anti-fraud organizations, collection agencies, and law enforcement agencies. this in order to make credit decisions, prosecute debtors, identify and prevent fraud, money laundering, terrorist financing and other financial crimes.

We would like to send you relevant and up-to-date information about our products and services by mail, phone, email, social media or SMS. You can inform us at any time of your refusal to receive such information from us by requesting it by e-mail or post, stating that you wish us to remove you from our distribution list by indicating your full name, mailing or email address and account details.

We will also use your personal information internally to conduct research and statistical studies to improve the quality of our services.

We may share your personal information collected online with people who provide us services (such as customer service) or who act as agents, if they undertake to respect the confidentiality of such information.

If you are one of our customers and would like to change or verify any information you have provided to us, please contact Customer Service.
